Overview

Organization is engaged in civil defense, protection of population and territories from emergency situations, fire safety, and safety of people on the water.

"There are still people who are ready to come to the aid of victims at any moment."

Task

To develop a portal in which anyone who cares could thank rescuers for the rescue or help, as well as encourage people to say thank you to the rescue services.

It has been done:

  • Web service
  • 5 unique interior pages
  • 7 images for the home screen that change randomly
  • A step-by-step thank-you form
  • Dynamic map with acknowledgements for 89 regions
  • All adapted for the phone
